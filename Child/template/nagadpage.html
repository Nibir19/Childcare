Send Money allows you to transfer money from your Nagad Account to another Nagad Account. To send money:
01. Go to your Mobile Menu by dialing *167#
02. Press 2 to select 'Send Money' option
03. Enter Our Nagad Account Number (+880171819140)
04. Enter the amount you want to send
05. Enter a reference about the transaction. (Do not use more than one word, avoid space or special characters)
06. Now enter your Nagad PIN to confirm the transaction
